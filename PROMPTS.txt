⏺ No errors. Here's what the camera now does:

  Zoom in (first 40% of scene): Camera starts at distance 48 and eases in to
  distance 30, creating a dramatic zoom into the contribution graph.

  Pan left to right (full duration): Camera X position sweeps from -10 to +10
  across the full scene, tracking the graph as tiles expand left to right.

  Subtle rotation: A small orbital offset (-2 to +2) is layered on top of the
  pan, giving the scene a gentle rotational drift as it moves.

  Look-at tracking: The focal point follows the pan at half speed (panX * 0.5),
  so the camera leads the look-at slightly — creating a natural parallax feel
  rather than a locked-on track.

  All motion uses Easing.inOut(Easing.quad) for smooth acceleration and
  deceleration.
